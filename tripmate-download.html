🆕 Organiser — setting up a new shared trip
1
Open app → tap Plan New Trip
2
Enter name, dates & who's coming → tap Next
3
Choose "I'm setting it up" → enter your X-MASTER-KEY → Create
4
Copy the sharing message → send to your group on WhatsApp
↩️ Friend — joining someone's trip
1
Open app → tap Join Trip
2
Enter the X-MASTER-KEY and Trip Code from the group chat
3
Tap Join & Sync → trip loads automatically
⚠️ Important — which key to use
Use the X-MASTER-KEY from jsonbin.io. Starts with $2a$10$. Do NOT use the Access Key (it has no permissions).
